resize to 1024x1024:
mogrify -resize 1024x1024 *.tga

resize to 360x360:
mogrify -resize 360x360 *.tga

resize to 164x224:
mogrify -resize 164x224 *.tga

resize to 328x448:
mogrify -resize 328x448 *.tga

tga to dds:
mogrify -format dds -define dds:mipmaps=10 -define dds:compression=dxt5 *.tga

png to tga:
mogrify -format tga *.png
