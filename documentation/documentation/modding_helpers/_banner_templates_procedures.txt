If the logo doesn't have blurred outlines, be sure to do that by doing the following
1. Alpha to selection
2. Shrink selection by 1
3. Apply gaussian blur 0.5
4. Select whole thing
5. Antialias
This makes the edges appear less pixelly and more natural looking on the banners

----- FOR ROMAN STRAT BANNER TEMPLATE
1. Colorize primary layers with faction's primary
2. Colorize secondary layers with faction's secondary
3. Resize faction's logo to 1024x1024
4. Go to channels
5. Channel to selection logo 848x848
6. Paste faction's logo into each layer with the channel selected for proper paste location
7. Adjust hard lights as necessary. Logo should stand out and be visible

----- FOR ROMAN BATTLE BANNER TEMPLATE
1. Colorize primary layers with faction's primary
2. Colorize secondary layers with faction's secondary
3. Resize faction's logo to 1280x1280
4. Go to channels
5. Channel to selection logo 1024x1024
6. Paste faction's logo into each layer with the channel selected for proper paste location
7. Adjust hard lights as necessary. Logo should stand out and be visible
8. For the left logo, do 960x960
